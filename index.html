About Us

Waffle Taco is a duo comprised of Ben Beauvais (bass, rhodes and vocals), and Kyle Linneman (trumpet and technology). influences include Portishead, Miles Davis, and Venetian snares.

Ben Beauvais is a singer/songwriter/multi instrumentalist/producer. He brings to the table over 30 years of performing, and recording experience on the LA and SF scenes. Ben plays a wide variety of styles and genres, combining aspects of funk, jazz, electronica, and hip hop to create a sophisticated yet visceral sound.

Kyle Linneman is an electronic musician/DJ/web programmer/trumpet performer. He has over 12 years of trumpet performance experience, and has a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. He plays trumpet in a wide variety of styles, but focuses his work on combining trumpet and electronics for unique and rich sound.

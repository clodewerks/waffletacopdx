Waffle Taco

“Aurally, wierdly delicious” Waffle Taco is a Portland based jazz, funk, and electronics duo. The duo consists of Ben Beauvais with bass, vocals, and electronics and Kyle Linneman on trumpet with electronics. Their sound has been described as Bitches Brew era Miles remixed. Waffle Taco has 2 albums and an EP, and has notably performed at NW LoopFest and OMSI.
